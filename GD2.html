SPRING 2024

Project 1: A Symbol of our Family

The objective of this exercise was to explore and understand the relationship between type, image & content, by visually representing our family in a contemporary and dynamic symbol, Coat of Arms or Family Shield.

Project 2: Image Matrix

Students were tasked with developing a “matrix” for this exercise consisting of several cells. Starting with the “Master” image (center cell). This image should be a visual interpretation of an abstract thought or feeling, mine being "Future".

Project 3: Book Cover Redesign

Through a series of permutations students were tasked with developing a visual narrative that engages the audience and communicates the content of 3 different, but related books. The book cover series directly relates to the previous theme of Project 2.

Project 4: Currency Redesign

For my currency redesign I decided to work with the Costa Rican Colon ₡. The vibrant colors and variety of animals depicted on the Costa Rican Colon is what caught my intrest initially. Using this as an oppurtunity to introduce more of Costa Rica's exotic animals.
